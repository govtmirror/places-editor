Looking for the beta of the Places Mobile Editor?

Places is an internal data collection system for the National Park Service's "core" geospatial data, including points of interest, buildings, roads, trails, and parking lots. Data from the system is used by many of the National Park Service's visitor-facing digital products like NPS.gov, Park Tiles, and Places Mobile iOS/Android apps.

Places was originally seeded with GIS datasets from parks, regions, and programs. For more information regarding these sources, take a look at this spreadsheet. If you would like to seed Places with a dataset, get in touch; we are happy to help!

Click on the "Places Editor" button above to start contributing to your park's map right now.
